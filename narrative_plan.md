## NeuGBI Slide Narrative Plan

### Audience
General English-speaking audience that needs a clear story, not a technical system walkthrough.

### Objective
Explain why traditional BI struggles with exploratory questions, use the Russia-Ukraine case as a concrete example, and introduce NeuGBI as a better way to conduct iterative analysis.

### Narrative Arc
1. Start with a familiar definition of traditional BI.
2. Show how BI is used in a real Russia-Ukraine analysis case.
3. Surface the key findings from that case.
4. Explain why the traditional workflow becomes heavy and repetitive.
5. Shift to a more iterative analysis rhythm.
6. Introduce NeuGBI as the core answer.
7. Close with how NeuGBI connects to an OpenClaw-style interface and broader information sources.

### Slide List
1. Title: From Traditional BI to NeuGBI
2. What Traditional BI Is
3. A Real BI Use Case: Russia-Ukraine and Russian Academia
4. What the Case Revealed
5. Why Traditional BI Starts to Break Down
6. A Better Rhythm: Iterative Exploration
7. What NeuGBI Is at Its Core
8. OpenClaw-Style Integration
